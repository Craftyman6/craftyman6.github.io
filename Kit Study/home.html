Welcome to the Kit Study site!

If you struggle with remembering the sub and special weapons assigned to all 143 weapons in Splatoon 3, you're in luck!

This site provides resources for honing your memory to all the kits in the game. Use the top navigation panel to explore the site's pages. These pages include:

Encyclopedia

A glorified version of the weapon select in-game, but right here on the site. If you need to brush up on the kit of a specific weapon, you've got it!

In addition, the encyclopedia displays whether a weapon shares the same kit with another weapon in pink on the bottom.

Kit Quizzer

Given a random sub and special, can you think of a weapon that has both?

This is the hardest of the three quizzers. If this isn't your speed, there's also:

Weapon Quizzer

Do you know the kit of a randomly given weapon? Take a guess and provide a sub and special that matches it.

Quick Quizzer

Are you ever unsure who it was on the enemy team that just threw a bomb at you? This quizzer prepares you for this exact situation.

Given a team of four weapons and a sub or special, can you identify the culprit?
