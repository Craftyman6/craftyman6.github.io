Hello from the middle of nowhere!

I've gotta thank my grandpa for having a birthday, he chose a perfect place to go for his get-together.

For three nights my mom's side of the family all stayed at the Camelot Ridge Resort Lodge in Indiana.

We used to go to places like this when we were little, and I remember them all being ginormous, so I expected this place to be smaller considering I've gotten bigger.

It was not

We ended up in a wonder, vast, and pretty damn epic lodge, and here I'd like to show you what I can of how the experience went!

The House

     The whole place was unreal. From top to bottom the entirity of its furnature, decorations, structure, and even simple cutlary was themed with wood and nature. 
     Even though we had 3 days to stay we didn't have enough meals to enjoy our food in every nook and cranny of the place. Places like the theater room, warmed light up swimming pool, exercise room (which Ali, Billie and I used as a bedroom), the, about, ten bathrooms, and even a whole ass western style saloon, all added to its nice charm.

The Family

     The Durbin side of my family is a real whacky one, but in a good way! (unlike a lot of my Schaffner side lmao). Four of the Six cousins were able to make it, along with my aunt, uncle, their spouces, grandpa, my grandma's sister and her spouce, and a few more people along the way.
     I could bet a whole hunk of money you'd get along real well with the Durbin side, especially with my crazy aunt Teri, can't go one moment without making some fun remark and laughing to herself.

The Stay

     As I said, Ali, Billie and I stayed in a gym room in the place, kinda wacky how there even was one in here, it was also upstairs which is kind of odd, right?
     They had a fold out couch to sleep on and I used a blow up mattress. And yes, even the gym room had a bathroom, just about every one did.
     Most of the stay there it was us three hanging out, especially at the pool, or we'd be eating, relaxing, struggling to get internet or gaming.

The Outside

     The whole place was one giant plot of land meant for hunting, but that doesn't mean it didn't go without its gorgeous sites!
     We took a tour across the whole plot and saw its forests, lakes, crops, dens, cabins, and much more! I'm glad I had the gut feeling to bring my phone to the tour to take some photos.

The Deer

   Deer :3 The place raised and owned plenty of deer that they keep on the land, the owners let us go out and feed them all and of course pet them too. The whole wildlife aspect that the lodge was going for perfectly accompanied this adorable animals!

The whole trip was incredible! Its something that I didn't even realize that I needed, it let off my worries and stress and it was a perfect bonding time with the rest of my family.

And now that I'm home it's time for the homestretch of working until school starts up! I actually am currently in the process of applying for Rhodes CCP so I can take Colege Algebra and ASL! I'm decently looking forward to school but at the same time not, and I'm sure you've heard that from both of our mouths enough already.

Keep eveything up, and I'll see you at the game Saturday and hopefully get a chance to see you Sunday!

But until then we've still got a week and a half to go, so lets keep looking up and forward to that. I know most of that time is, still, going to work and back for you, and I believe after starting school it'll be a lot more relieving for your soul to not be stuck doing the same thing over and over, barely talking and only working.

I love youuuuuuu <3
